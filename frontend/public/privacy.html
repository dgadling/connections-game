Privacy Policy — Connections Game

We store your Discord ID, Discord username, global display name, and avatar hash — public profile data from Discord's identify scope. We also store encrypted Discord OAuth access and refresh tokens, used for silent auto-login. Tokens are deleted when you log out.

Game content (member names, questions, pairing history) is visible only to admins of that specific game.

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.
